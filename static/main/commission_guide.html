How to Commission a Drawing

Back to Home Try the Budget Simulator

Step 1: Gather Your Ideas

To ensure I can create the perfect piece for you, please prepare the following details before contacting me. The more specific you are, the better the final result will be!

Drawing Style (Rendering Type)

  • Sketch: Chargement...
  • Lineart: Chargement...
  • Flat: Chargement...
  • Render: Chargement...

Character detail

  • Head: Head and neck only.
  • Chest: From the head down to the chest.
  • Full: Full body drawing.

Additional detail (optional)

  • Background: Do you want a simple background or a detailed environment?
  • Extra Character(s): 25% of the original price will be added for each extra character.

Step 2: Prepare Your References

Visual aids can be helpful! Gather any materials that illustrate your vision. You can use:

  • Reference Images: Pictures of characters, outfits, poses, or expressions.
  • Written Description: I think it's self-explanatory :)

Step 3: Contact Me

Once you have gathered all the necessary information, send me a detailed commission request! Please include everything from step 1 and step 2 in your message. You can contact me on Tumblr or Instagram

We will discuss the specifics then proceed with half the payment! I'll update you on your commission and will request the other half payment once I'm finished.
